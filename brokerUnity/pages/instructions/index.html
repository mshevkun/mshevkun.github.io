Withdrawal

Interactive Trade aims to provide you with ease and comfort when it comes to withdrawing your funds. We are always striving to give our clients the best service, so we seek to process all withdrawal requests made within 24 hours.

A withdrawal will only be accepted if the appropriate documentation has been provided so that the Client's trading account is fully verified. Withdrawals should be made using the same method used to fund your trading account.

Withdrawing funds from your Interactive Trade account is a simple process. You will need to log in to your personal area, select the account you want to make a withdrawal from, click on the withdrawal option, and then follow the instructions to fill in all fields to complete the process.

Deposit

At Interactive Trade, we have provided the option of wire transfer and collaborated with leading and secure payment providers, such as Visa and MasterCard, to allow our clients to make deposits using the most convenient method for them. Clients’ funds’ safety is of vital importance for Interactive Trade. That is why a personalized and sophisticated client portal has been developed to give flexibility and comfort to our clients while processing secure deposits and withdrawals from a single source.

Depositing funds into your Interactive Trade account is an easy process.
1. You need to log in to your personal area.
2. Choose the account you want to deposit in.
3. Click on the deposit option.
4. Select your preferred method for deposit.   You will then need to follow the instructions and fill in all fields that will be offered to you to complete a deposit.

Please note that the trading account owner can only make deposits. We strive to make depositing simple and quick for our traders. Please contact us if you have any suggestions.
